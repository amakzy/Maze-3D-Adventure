Features

3D Raycasting

Navigate through the maze using first-person perspective rendered with efficient raycasting technology.

First-Person Movement

Control your character's movement through the maze with keyboard or mouse, just like classic FPS games.

Collision Detection

Avoid walking through walls with accurate collision detection that keeps you on track in the maze.

About

This project was inspired by classic 3D games like Wolfenstein 3D. We wanted to bring the nostalgia of retro first-person experiences to a new generation, focusing on maze exploration and 3D rendering.

This project was developed as part of the Holberton School Portfolio Project. Check out more details on our journey here.
